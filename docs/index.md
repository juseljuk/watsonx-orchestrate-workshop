# Bobchestrate Workshop

Welcome to the Bobchestrate Workshop! This hands-on workshop will guide you through building AI agents with IBM watsonx Orchestrate.

![Workshop Logo](Bobchestrate_Workshop_logo_new.png)

## Workshop Overview

This workshop is designed to take you from setup to deployment, covering all aspects of building production-ready AI agents.

## Workshop Parts

### [Part 1: Setup](part1-setup/README.md)
Get your development environment ready and configure all necessary tools.

### [Part 2: First Agent](part2-first-agent/README.md)
Build your first AI agent and understand the fundamentals.

### [Part 2b: Bob Custom Rules](part2b-bob-custom-rules/README.md)
Learn how to customize Bob with your own rules and guidelines.

### [Part 3: Custom Tools](part3-custom-tools/README.md)
Create custom tools to extend your agent's capabilities.

### [Part 3b: AI Gateway Models](part3b-ai-gateway-models/README.md)
Explore different AI models and learn when to use each one.

### [Part 4: Knowledge](part4-knowledge/README.md)
Integrate knowledge bases to make your agents more intelligent.

### [Part 5: Guidelines & Guardrails](part5-guidelines-guardrails/README.md)
Implement safety measures and operational guidelines.

### [Part 6: MCP Servers](part6-mcp-servers/README.md)
Build Model Context Protocol servers for advanced integrations.

### [Part 7: Deployment](part7-deployment/README.md)
Deploy your agents to production environments.

### [Part 8: Multi-Agent Orchestration](part8-multi-agent-orchestration/README.md)
Coordinate multiple agents to solve complex problems.
